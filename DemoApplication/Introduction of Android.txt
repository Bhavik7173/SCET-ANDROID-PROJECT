Android Life Cycle
1.onCreate - called when activity is first created.
2.onStart - when activity is becoming visible to the user.
3.onResume - when activity will start interacting with the user.
4.onPause - when activityis not visible to the user or going to background.
5.onStop - when activity is no longer visibleto the user
6.on Restart - when activity is stopped and after again open it.
7.onDestroy - before the activityis destroy in background.
